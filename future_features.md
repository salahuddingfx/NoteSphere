# NoteSphere Future Roadmap 🚀

This document outlines potential features and enhancements to take NoteSphere to the next level as a premium academic vault.

## 1. Academic & Note Management
*   **AI Note Summarizer**: Automatically generate short summaries of long PDFs or notes using Gemini/OpenAI.
*   **Smart OCR**: Allow users to upload photos of handwritten notes and convert them into searchable text.
*   **In-Browser PDF Annotation**: Allow students to highlight and add sticky notes directly on shared PDFs.
*   **Advanced Filter/Search**: Search by Semester, Subject Code, Teacher, or even specific keywords inside documents.
*   **Plagiarism Checker**: Ensure the quality and originality of uploaded notes.

## 2. Gamification & Engagement (XP 2.0)
*   **Note Streaks**: Rewards for users who upload or study every day.
*   **Virtual Shop**: Use "Study Points" or XP to unlock custom profile cards, unique badges, or dark mode themes.
*   **Contribution Levels**: Different roles like "Novice Contributor," "Verified Scholar," and "Academic Legend."
*   **Leaderboard Leagues**: Weekly "Top Contributor" rankings with visible rewards on the profile.

## 3. Social & Collaboration
*   **Study Groups**: Create private or public groups for specific subjects where students can discuss and share resources.
*   **Real-time Q&A**: A forum where students can ask questions and others can answer, with the best answer marked by the moderator.
*   **Mentorship Program**: A system to connect seniors who have mastered a subject with juniors who need help.
*   **Live Collaborative Whiteboard**: For real-time group study sessions.

## 4. Technical & UX Enhancements
*   **PWA (Progressive Web App)**: Full offline support so students can read their "Saved Notes" even without internet.
*   **Mobile App (React Native/Flutter)**: A dedicated mobile experience for on-the-go studying.
*   **Dark/Light/System Themes**: Highly polished UI themes with glassmorphism effects.
*   **Multilingual Support**: Toggle between English and Bengali for better accessibility.
*   **Voice Search**: Search for notes using voice commands.

## 5. Admin & Analytics
*   **Analytics Dashboard**: Insights for admins to see which subjects are most popular and who the most active users are.
*   **Automated Content Moderation**: Use AI to flag inappropriate or low-quality uploads before they go live.
*   **Feedback Loop**: A system for users to report errors in notes or suggest improvements directly to the uploader.

---

> [!TIP]
> **Priority Suggestion**: Starting with **PWA support** and **AI Summarization** would give NoteSphere a massive competitive advantage and provide immediate value to students.
